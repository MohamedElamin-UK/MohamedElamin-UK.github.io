Garlic Butter Pasta

  • Time: 25 minutes
  • Serves: 4
Ingredients Quantity
Pasta 250g
Garlic cloves 3Pcs
Butter 50g
Parsley 2 tbsp
Salt To taste

Step 1: Cook the pasta according to package instructions. Drain and set aside.

Step 2: Melt butter in a pan over medium heat. Add minced garlic and sauté until fragrant.

Step 3: Add the cooked pasta to the pan. Toss to coat with butter and garlic.

Step 4: Garnish with chopped parsley and season with salt. Serve warm.

Creamy Mushroom Risotto

  • Time: 40 minutes
  • Serves: 4
Ingredients Quantity
Arborio rice 1 cup
Mushrooms 200g
Chicken or vegetable stock 4 cups
Parmesan cheese 1/2 cup
Butter 2 tbsp

Step 1: Sauté sliced mushrooms in butter until tender and set aside.

Step 2: In a pot, heat stock and keep it warm.

Step 3: In another pan, sauté Arborio rice in butter for 2-3 minutes. Gradually add stock, one ladle at a time, stirring continuously until absorbed.

Step 4: Mix in the cooked mushrooms and Parmesan cheese. Serve hot.

Classic Margherita Pizza

  • Time: 30 minutes (prep) + 15 minutes (bake)
  • Serves: 2
Ingredients Quantity
Pizza dough 1 (12-inch crust)
Tomato sauce 1/2 cup
Fresh mozzarella 200g
Fresh basil leaves Handful
Olive oil 2 tbsp

Step 1: Preheat the oven to 220°C (430°F). Roll out the pizza dough on a baking tray.

Step 2: Spread tomato sauce evenly over the crust.

Step 3: Add slices of fresh mozzarella and bake for 10-15 minutes, or until the crust is golden and the cheese is bubbling.

Step 4: Garnish with fresh basil leaves and drizzle with olive oil before serving.
